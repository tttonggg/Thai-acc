## Outreach Templates

### Template: Link Building Email
```
Subject: [Article Title] — potential for [their site]

Hi [Name],

I came across your article on [topic] and found it really [specific compliment].

I recently published a guide on [related topic] that might complement your content: [URL]

Specifically, I thought [specific section] might resonate with your readers who are interested in [their audience].

Would you consider adding a link to my article from your [specific section]?

[Your name]
```

```
Hi [Name],

I noticed [their recent article on topic] and your readers seem engaged with [specific point].

I'd like to contribute a guest post on [unique angle] — something I think would [specific value for their audience].

Outline:
- H2: [Point 1]

Would you be open to this?

[Your name]
```
