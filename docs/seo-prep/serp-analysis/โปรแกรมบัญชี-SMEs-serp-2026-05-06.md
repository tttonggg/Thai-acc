### Content Gaps (Your Opportunities)

1. **SME size-specific recommendation engine** — No competitor maps business size to specific plan/features. "5 คน ขายของออนไลน์ 2 ร้าน" vs "2 คน บริการลูกค้า" need different things. Opportunity: a decision tree or quiz.

2. **Real-time financial health dashboard for SMEs** — Competitors show static feature lists; none show a live CFO dashboard preview. Opportunity: a visual "what your dashboard looks like on Keerati" that competitors don't provide.

3. **Switching/migration support** — No competitor addresses "I'm already using [competitor], how hard is it to switch?" — a common SME fear. Opportunity: a migration guide or "switch in 30 minutes" promise.

### PAA Questions

1. โปรแกรมบัญชี SMEs ราคาเท่าไหร่? — Ranges from free (FlowAccount) to ~200-500 THB/month depending on features.
2. โปรแกรมบัญชี SMEs ดีกว่า Excel อย่างไร? — Automation of tax calculations, multi-user access, and audit trails that Excel cannot provide.
3. ต้องเสียค่าใช้จ่ายอะไรอีกนอกจากค่าบอก? — Consider: per-user fees, e-commerce integrations, tax form submissions, implementation support.

### What to Beat

- **From #1 (FlowAccount):** Their accounting partner network (5,700+ firms) is a moat — Keerati needs to build a similar ecosystem or differentiate on price/quality for the same accountant audience.
- **From #2 (PEAK):** Their LINE Connect real-time notifications is a strong differentiator for SME owners. Keerati needs a comparable (or better) mobile/notification story.

---

### Beat Plan

1. **Build an SME-specific landing page** with a "which plan" selector — current competitors treat SMEs as a generic segment.
2. **Create a "day in the life" visual** — show 5 SME workflows (morning invoice, lunchtime bank match, end-of-day VAT report) in Keerati — visual storytelling competitors lack.
3. **Add a "why SMEs choose Keerati over [competitor]" comparison** — addressing the switching question directly.
4. **Feature a named CPA/thai accountant endorsement** — E-E-A-T signal competitors miss on their main landing pages.
